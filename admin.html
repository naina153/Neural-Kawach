How to Deal with a Cyber Attack

  • Immediately disconnect the affected device from the internet.
  • Do not share OTPs, passwords, or banking details with anyone.
  • Change passwords of all important accounts.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
  • Report the incident to official authorities immediately.

Privacy & Data Protection Guidelines

  • Use strong and unique passwords for each service.
  • Do not click suspicious links in SMS or emails.
  • Verify website URLs before making online payments.
  • Keep your operating system and apps updated.
  • Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
